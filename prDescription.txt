PR Title: FEAT/FEAPP-420: WhatsApp Notification Option for Product Updates

PR Description:

WHAT:
    This PR provides a feature to customers to receive notifications via WhatsApp in addition to their email notifications
    for product updates. Users can now add their WhatsApp number as a notification method. When enabled, notifications
    about product updates will be sent to both the given email and WhatsApp number.

WHY:
    This feature is being added in response to customer requests for more flexibility in how they receive notifications about product
    updates. By allowing notifications via WhatsApp, we aim to enhance user engagement and meet the preferences of our customers.
